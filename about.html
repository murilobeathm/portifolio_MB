A little about me...


I'm Brazilian and currently live in Portugal. When I lived in Brazil I worked as a Naval Marine - Marinha Do Brasil, but I wasn't happy with my career so I decided to change my life and start over. I moved to Portugal in 2019 with the intention of studying and changing careers, but I still didn't know which one. I worked in a restaurant for two years to save money for my studies, when I already had a considerable amount I decided to join a Java programming academy at Rumos, on the recommendation of a colleague who is a developer, and shortly after finishing this course I took another in database development at Cinel. But I couldn't get into the field straight away, despite having invested so much time and money, because my initial goal was to become a Java programmer. Almost two years later, the same colleague advised me on where and how to study, saw that I was still working hard and hadn't given up, and sent me for an interview for training in Quality Assurance (thanks Mumu). I got the job and got to know the profession that really instigated me and excites me every day, with every new tool I learn.
I'm a software tester.
